“ give a man a fish and feed him for a day “
“ teach a man to fish and feed him for a lifetime “

either that or he sits in a boat drinking beer all day

New Zealand is a world class Fishing Location. Our waters are second to none when it comes to an incredible variety of fish species, including Snapper, trevally, terakihi, kingfish, kahawai, Gurnard, Blue Cod, blue moki, john dory, Hapuka and plenty more.

We are also a first-class big game fishing location, as our seas are harbouring plenty of big game fish such as Marlin, sword fish, Tuna, and the Mighty Yellowtail Kingfish to name a few.

And let’s not forget the superb trout and Salmon fishing in the pristine, shimmering lakes and rivers throughout our amazing country.

With over 15,000 KM’s of stunning coastline ( the 10th longest in the world) and the 4th largest fishing zone worldwide it’s no wonder over a million kiwis love to get out for a fish every year.

Let me ask you this..

would you like to…

  • discover all the prime tips tricks and techniques for catching more fish
  • connect with other spirited fisherman, share your passion, knowledge, and expertise
  • Stay up to date with the latest news and fishing forum discussions
  • Share your stories, adventures, and photos with other anglers throughout New Zealand and the world
  • Gain access to all our fishing resources, bite times, knot guide, tide charts, forum etc
  • join or start your own group, find a new fishing buddy and form new friendships
  • become part of a community that is passionate about everything fishing related
  • And so much more…

If you answered yes to any of the above then You will find a home here at NZ FISHING CLUB
Join the ranks of enthusiastic fishermen in New Zealand today and share your love for fishing with like minded individuals.
We look forward to seeing your pictures or videos and hearing a little about you and your adventures.
